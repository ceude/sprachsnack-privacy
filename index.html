Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

SprachSnack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our iOS application SprachSnack: Deutsche Wörter.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Account data: username and profile information you provide during registration.
  • Usage data: vocabulary progress, quiz results, streaks, and in-app activity.
  • Purchase data: subscription and in-app purchase records processed by Apple.
  • Device data: device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostics.
  • Communication data: messages sent through the in-app chat feature.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app functionality.
  • To sync your progress across sessions via our backend (Supabase).
  • To send push notifications (only if you grant permission).
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob (free tier only).
  • To process subscription payments via Apple's StoreKit.

3. Advertising and Tracking

The free version of SprachSnack displays ads provided by Google AdMob. In the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, we use the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP SDK) to obtain your consent before personalised advertising is enabled, in compliance with GDPR. We do not use the Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework for cross-app tracking purposes on Apple devices.

Premium subscribers do not see ads.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your information. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

SprachSnack is rated 17+ and is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. If you are in the EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR including access, rectification, and portability of your data.
